# Beginner

!!! tip "What is Beginner?"
    **Beginner** is a standalone menu for newcomers that works without any settings or presets. You don't even need to save birth data first — just enter it on the spot and start reading right away. By reading straight down the page, you naturally learn how to read your own birth chart (natal chart).

!!! note "No settings needed"
    Unlike other charts, Beginner does not require you to choose a preset. The displayed planets and aspects are fixed for beginners, so you can focus on *reading* without worrying about settings.

## Birth data and chart

### Steps

1. Open "**Beginner**" at the top of the main menu.
2. Prepare the birth data. Either **choose from the picker in the header** or **type it in directly** on the spot (you don't need to register it in advance — the chart below is ready to read immediately).
3. A large **single chart (natal chart)** appears just below. Use the "**Change color**" button on the upper right to switch the color scheme (standard pastel / old-StarNavigator purple) with one tap.
4. From the "**Open interpretation report**" button right under the chart, you can open a detailed report (Plus plan or higher).
5. Scroll further down for "**Read your nature**" and "**Read by theme**".

### Saving the entered birth data

- Pressing "**Save as new**" below the input fields saves the entered birth data as **registered (main) birth data**. It then appears in the header picker and the "Birth data list", and can be used as-is in other charts such as the single or double chart.
- If you recalled a saved person and edited their details, use "**Overwrite**" to update that record (use "Save as new" to register them as a different person).
- The "Save as new" / "Overwrite" buttons appear **when you are logged in (Free plan or higher)**.
- The "**Show chart**" button only recalculates — it does not save. Use the buttons above when you want to save.

## Read your nature

Below the chart are sections for reading the nature you were born with.

### Planets and signs

- **The Sun, Moon, and Ascendant (ASC)** are shown as large cards with reading guides.
- Other planets (Mercury, Venus, Mars, …) are listed; **click one to see its meaning**.
- If the birth time is unknown, the ASC cannot be determined, so the ASC card is not shown.

### Balance of the three and four divisions

- The balance of Fire, Earth, Air, Water (four elements) and Cardinal, Fixed, Mutable (three qualities) is shown with explanations.

## Read by theme

When the birth time is known, "theme chips" for reading by theme are shown.

### Steps

1. Choose one of the **theme chips** (Work, Money, Love & Marriage, Health, Family, Relationships & Communication — 6 in total).
2. A guide opens below the chip, showing the houses, ruling planets, and planets to focus on for that theme, filled in with your chart's own values.
3. At the same time, the rows and columns of the highlighted planets are colored in the **aspect grid just below**. **Click** a highlighted cell to see the meaning of that aspect.
4. Press again to clear it. Pressing another theme switches over (only one theme is lit at a time).

!!! info "Plans, and when the birth time is unknown"
    - For the theme feature, Free users can try only the "Work" theme; the remaining 5 themes require Basic or higher.
    - If the birth time is unknown, houses cannot be determined, so the theme feature is not shown. Instead, you can practice by clicking cells in the aspect grid to read their meanings.

## Glossary

- Open the "**Glossary**" from the button at the lower right of the screen.
- It has 3 tabs: **12 Signs / 12 Houses / 5 Aspects**. When unsure how to read something, check here.
